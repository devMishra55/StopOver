Cookie Policy

To provide you with the best possible experience on our StopOverSky travel platform, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies. These small data files help our website remember your preferences, analyze how you interact with our services, and deliver personalized content tailored to your travel interests.

We utilize different types of cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Essential cookies keep our platform secure and enable core functions like account logins and booking sessions. Performance cookies give us insights into how visitors use our website, allowing us to optimize its design and functionality. Preference cookies remember your selected language, currency, and other settings to create a more convenient experience. When you're exploring travel options, marketing cookies help us show you relevant offers and destinations that match your interests.

You have full control over your cookie preferences. Most web browsers allow you to manage or disable cookies through their settings menu. Please note that if you choose to block essential cookies, some features of our platform may not work properly. For a seamless booking experience, we recommend keeping cookies enabled.

This Cookie Policy works alongside our comprehensive Privacy Policy to ensure transparency about how we handle your data. If you have any questions about our use of cookies or your privacy options, our customer support team is always available to assist you.
