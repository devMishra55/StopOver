Privacy Policy

At Skywings, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to transparently handling your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our travel booking services.

When you create an account or make bookings, we collect essential details like your name, contact information, and payment data—all secured through encryption. We also gather travel preferences and technical data (like IP addresses) to enhance your experience and platform functionality.

Your information helps us process bookings efficiently, personalize travel recommendations, and improve our services. We may share necessary details with trusted travel partners and payment processors solely to fulfill your reservations. Rest assured, we never sell your data to third-party marketers.

You retain control over your information: access, update, or delete your account anytime, opt out of promotional emails, or manage cookie preferences via your browser. Our robust security measures include SSL encryption and restricted data access to protect your privacy.

We may update this policy periodically, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance. For questions or data requests, contact our Privacy Team at privacy@skywings.com or +1 (800) 555-SKYW.
